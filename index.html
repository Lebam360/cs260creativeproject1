About this cook book!

This is a digitalized family cookbook. Food is a great way to bring people together. Great great aunt Suzie always dreamed that we could keep her traditions going even after she passed. She dreamed that we could come together as a family and enjoy lovely meals. She also hoped that we could continue making these delicious family recipes in our home and share the stories of where they came from. That's when Cooking Made Easy was born! We have gathered all these magnificient recipes in one place to share with the coming generations.


In this website you will find:

  • Appetizers and snacks
  • Main course dishes
  • And yummy desserts!
