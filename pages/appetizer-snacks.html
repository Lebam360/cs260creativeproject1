Appetizers and Snacks

Artichoke Dip

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces cream cheese
  • 1 can artichoke hearts
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 (4 oz.) can diced green chilies
  • 1 clove garlic, minced

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. Mix all ingredients together with mixer.
  3. Bake 15 minutes.

Fresh Salsa

Ingredients:

  • 6 regular chopped tomatoes
  • 6 chopped tomatillos
  • 1/2 chopped white onion
  • 5 chopped jalapenos (seeded as needed for hotness)
  • Juice of 1/2 orange
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

  1. Combine all ingredients.
  2. Enjoy!
