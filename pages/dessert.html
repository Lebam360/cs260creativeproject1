Desserts

Banana Cream Pudding

Ingredients:

  • banana pudding
  • vanilla wafers
  • sliced bananas
  • whipped cream

Directions:

  1. Prepare the banana pudding.
  2. Put a layer of vanilla wafers on the bottom of a large bowl.
  3. Top the layer of vanilla wafers with a layer of banana pudding.
  4. Carefully put the banana slices on top of the pudding to make another layer
  5. Spread whipped cream on top of the banana layer.
  6. Repeat steps 2-5 until the bowl is filled, ending with a whipped cream layer.
  7. Enjoy!

Banana Bread

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Directions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit. Lightly grease one 9x5 inch pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda and salt.
  3. Combine sugar, eggs, and bananas in a separate bowl.
  4. Stir wet mixture into the dry mixture.
  5. Pour the mixture into the pan and bake for 60 to 65 minutes. If you can poke a toothpick into the center of the loaf and it comes out clean, the bread is done. If the toothpick does not come out clean, cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Let the bread cool and Enjoy!
