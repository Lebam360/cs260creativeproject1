Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 tbsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp corn flour
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1-2 tsp cayenne (more=hotter)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 16oz can red kidney beans
  • 1 16oz can pinto beans
  • 1 16oz can black beans
  • 1/2 16oz can whole kernel corn
  • 1/2 16oz can garbanzo beans
  • 2 lbs. ground beef, raw
  • 1 large onion, cubed
  • 1 28oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 6oz can tomato paste
  1. Mix the spices together in a small bowl.
  2. Put the ground beef into a skillet. Mix in half of the spices and fry on high, mixing often, until the meat is browned.
  3. Add onion to the beef and cook until the onions have become clear.
  4. Mix the beans, tomato sauce and tomato paste in a large pot and turn the stove to high. Mix in the rest of the spices. Stir reguarly until mixture starts to bubble. Reduce heat and simmer.
  5. Once the mixture is warm, mix in the beef and onion mix. Let simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!

Roast

Ingredients:

  • 3 1lb chuck roast
  • 2 tbsp steak seasoning
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 lb of baby carrots
  • 1 chopped potatoes
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2-3 stalks of celery
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • minced parsley (optional) to garnish
  1. Add everything except the parsley to your crock pot and cover, cooking on low for 8-10 hours or on high for 5-6 hours
  2. Garnesh the roast with the parsley.
  3. Enjoy!
